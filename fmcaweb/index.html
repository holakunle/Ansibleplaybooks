OUR MISSION

By efficiently applying Electronic Medical
Records in the Centre, the EMR Unit shall
reduce patient waiting time, ensure data
quality and security and block all financial
leakages.

OUR VISION

The EMR Unit aims to provide quality
and timely clinical services to patients
while boosting revenue generation at
Federal Medical Centre in Abeokuta.

Dr. OLUWASANMI KEHINDE

Head of Unit

Introduction

The Hospital Management Committee approved the establishment of an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Unit under the supervision of the Medical Director on the 12 th of July 2022, to strengthen the EMR implementation process and ensure its perfect adoption in the Centre. This unit is headed by Dr O.O Kehinde and supported by 18 other staff drawn from various departments including HIM, ICT, Accounts, Audit, Laboratory and Pharmacy among others.
